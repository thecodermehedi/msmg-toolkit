
- Open the URL : https://store.rg-adguard.net/

- Change URL (link) to PackageFamilyName

- Inside Sample Data textbox, Enter the Appx Package Family Name

- Choose RP (Release Preview) or Fast Channel

- Click on Ok

- The list of Apps available will be displayed, download the required .appx or .appxbundle file.

- Copy the downloaded .appx or .appxbundle file to Toolkit's Packs\Apps\<OS> folder.
